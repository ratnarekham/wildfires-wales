Small Country, Big Problem

In 2025, Wales experienced the highest wildfire impact compared to the rest of the UK. Despite being much smaller than Scotland and England, Wales has the second-highest absolute burned area this year. It also bears the brunt relative to its size, with the highest burned area per hectare.
